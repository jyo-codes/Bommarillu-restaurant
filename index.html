Welcome to our restaurant! We serve the best food in the world! Bommarillu Restaurant is celebrated for its authentic South Indian and Andhra-style cuisine, offering a variety of vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes. Signature offerings include biryanis and spicy curries, all prepared with a focus on traditional flavors and homely recipes. The restaurant provides a cozy, family-friendly ambiance, making it an ideal spot for casual dining

Committed to quality, Bommarillu sources fresh, high-quality ingredients to ensure each dish delivers a truly homely experience. They also offer complimentary delivery services for added convenience.

In addition to dining, Bommarillu Family Restaurant and Function Hall in Miyapur, Hyderabad, serves as a venue for various events, including weddings and receptions. The facility can accommodate up to 550 guests and offers amenities like valet parking and air-conditioned halls, catering to both vegetarian and non-vegetarian preferences.
